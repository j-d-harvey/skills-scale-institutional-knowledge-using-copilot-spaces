## Communication Principles

1. **Transparency:** Share both good news and challenges openly
2. **Timeliness:** Communicate early and often, especially on issues
3. **Relevance:** Tailor message depth to stakeholder interest level
4. **Two-Way:** Encourage questions and create space for feedback
5. **Consistency:** Maintain regular cadence so stakeholders know what to expect

---

## Escalation Path

### Level 1: Team-Level Resolution
**Examples:** Minor delays, resource questions, tactical decisions  
**Owner:** Project Manager  
**Resolution Time:** 1-2 days

### Level 2: Stakeholder Consultation
**Examples:** Scope changes, schedule adjustments, priority conflicts  
**Owner:** Stakeholder Liaison + Product Manager  
**Resolution Time:** 3-5 days

### Level 3: Executive Decision
**Examples:** Major budget overruns, critical resource gaps, strategic pivots  
**Owner:** Executive Sponsor  
**Resolution Time:** 1 week

## Success Metrics for Communication

- **Stakeholder Satisfaction:** Measured via quarterly survey (target: >80% satisfaction)
- **Response Rate:** % of stakeholders engaging with updates (target: >70%)
- **Escalation Rate:** # of surprise escalations (target: <2 per quarter)
- **Meeting Attendance:** % attendance at key stakeholder meetings (target: >85%)
